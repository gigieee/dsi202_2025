About Us

PawPal is a community-driven project dedicated to rescuing and rehoming stray dogs and cats. Our mission is to provide second chances for abandoned animals through care, compassion, and connection with loving families.

We collaborate with volunteers, vets, and kind-hearted people across the country to ensure that no animal is left behind. Our platform makes it easy to explore adoptable pets, donate for medical treatments, or support through your time and voice.

Whether you're here to adopt a furry friend or contribute to their well-being, PawPal welcomes you to be part of this life-saving journey. Together, we can make a lasting impact.

Our Vision

A world where every stray animal finds a home and a heart to belong to.

Our Mission

Rescue, treat, and rehome abandoned animals while educating communities on responsible pet care.
